Keeping your dog safe while you're away

About

Astro is a robot companion for dogs and dog owners that allows dog owners to feed, let out, and play with their dogs remotely.

Dog owners who work or travel often are concerned about the daily interactions that dogs need to be healthy such as feeding, going outside, and exercising. Dogs left alone can suffer from boredom, stress, and other health issues.

Astro is an innovative product that tackles this problem space and provides a one-in-all solution for dog owners.


With Astro, dog owners can easily communicate, take care of, and enhance their dog's lives when the dog owners are away.

Product Features

Feeding

Astro is able to feed your dog remotely through manual controls or a scheduled event. The robot can hold up to 6 pounds of dry dog food!

Letting In and Out

With its advanced robotic arm and sensors, Astro is able to open and close any type of door. Astro can let dogs in and out, using its sensors to make sure dogs are inside and outside safely.

Monitoring

The 360 degrees camera with 1080HD quality allows dog owners to check up on their dogs at anytime. Astro even records specific motions and events based on the dog owner's preferences and stores them in the cloud.

Maintaining Health

Astro helps dogs with boredom at home! The robot is able to play with dogs using smart toys and its robotic arm. It can play tug of war and fetch, using its sensors to safely play inside the house.
