Keep Your Medical History Close With World Record

The Problem

What happens when you get sick abroad? Without medical records on file and an added language barrier to overcome, most people are stuck in dire need of help. There is a need for people to have their medical records on them while traveling, as well as translated to the language of their destination.

Our Solution

World Record is a concealable storage device that contains your medical records in a safe and secure way. The medicals records can be translated to any language around the world.

Features

Small & Secure

With a small form factor and biometric fingerprint scanning, World Record secures your medical history in a lightweight package.

Translation

World Record translates your medical records into any language around the world to aid communication with foreign doctors.

Custom Notes

Users can log notes into World Record, whether to track their daily health or use it as a travel diary.
