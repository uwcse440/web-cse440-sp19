What is Cabin?

About Cabin

Cabin is a 3-piece set of accessories that is designed to help truckers and their families feeling more connected passively while the trucker is on the road. The design includes: a house ornament, for the trucker to place on their dashboard, a truck ornament for the family to use to communicate with the trucker, and a dashboard clicker, for the trucker to use to communicate with the family.

The main feature of Cabin is the lighting feature: when the family turns on the lights in their home, the corresponding lights in the house ornament will light up. Similarly, if the trucker turns his/her light on, that behavior is reflected in the truck ornament.

Other Features Include:

  • Sending dashcam videos of scenery to the family
  • Automatically-adjusting lighting levels (dim mode at night)
  • Viewing videos sent from trucker
  • Ability to mark oneself as available for a call
  • Embedded hard drive to store videos before sending/viewing
